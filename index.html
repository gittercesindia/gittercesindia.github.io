    DMT

    DMT (Domestic Money Transfer) services enable individuals to send money from one bank account to another within the country, typically through agents or online platforms.

    Money Transfer Services

    Money Transfer Services allow individuals to send and receive money securely and quickly across different locations, either locally or internationally. These services are commonly used for personal remittances, and bill payments.

    Mobile Recharge Services

    Mobile recharge services allow users to add credit to their mobile phones for talk time, data, or other services. This service provides a quick and easy way to recharge prepaid mobile connections for any network provider.

    BBPS Bill Payments

    BBPS (Bharat Bill Payment System) is an integrated bill payment service that allows users to pay utility bills such as electricity, water, gas, and more. It provides a secure and convenient way to settle bills online across multiple service providers.

    AEPS

    AEPS (Aadhaar Enabled Payment System) is a payment system that allows users to perform financial transactions using their Aadhaar number and biometric authentication (fingerprint or iris scan). AEPS is primarily used for bank-to-bank money transfers, balance inquiries, mini-statements, and other financial services through Aadhaar-linked bank accounts.
